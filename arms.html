Firearm Caliber Comparison

Caliber Description
.223 Remington A rifle caliber primarily used in rifles such as the AR-15 platform for varmint hunting and target shooting.
9mm Luger A versatile and popular pistol caliber used for self-defense, law enforcement, and competitive shooting.
7.62x25mm Tokarev A high-velocity pistol caliber originally developed for Soviet military use, known for penetration.
9x18mm Makarov A Russian pistol caliber commonly used in the Makarov pistol, offering moderate recoil and reliability.
9x21mm A caliber developed to meet specific legal requirements in regions where restrictions on military calibers like 9mm Luger exist.
9mm Browning (9mm Browning Long) An older cartridge primarily used in vintage or historical firearms, featuring lower pressure and velocity compared to 9mm Luger.
